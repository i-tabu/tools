Effective Date: August 1, 2025

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All processing is done locally in your browser.

If any i-Tabu Tool uses Google Sign-In, it is only for authentication purposes. No personal data (such as your email or profile) is stored or shared by us.

3. Audio and File Data

For tools like the Voice Recorder, all recordings are handled locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to a server.

This site does not use cookies or analytics services.
